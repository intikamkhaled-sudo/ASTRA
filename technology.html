SYSTEMS & TECHNOLOGY

Our Capture Systems

Two complementary technologies designed to handle debris of any size in low Earth orbit.

SYSTEM 01 — SMALL DEBRIS

Tether Net
System

The tether net is a compact, foldable mesh deployed at high velocity toward small debris objects. Guided by the satellite's onboard AI camera, the net adjusts its trajectory autonomously to intercept the target debris with precision.

Once the debris is enclosed, the net contracts and tethers the object to the satellite. A controlled deorbit maneuver then redirects both satellite and debris into a descending trajectory, where the captured material burns up completely on atmospheric reentry.

Target Size1m – 30m
GuidanceAI Camera + Computer Vision
DisposalAtmospheric Burnup

SYSTEM 02 — LARGE DEBRIS

Robotic Arm +
Electromagnet

When debris exceeds a critical size threshold, the AI routes control to the robotic arm system. The arm extends from the satellite body and activates a powerful electromagnetic head that attracts metallic debris — drawing it in for a secure mechanical grip.

With the debris locked in place, the arm repositions the object into an Earth-crossing trajectory. Released at the right vector, the debris enters the atmosphere and disintegrates safely, eliminating the orbital threat completely.

Target Size50cm+
MechanismRobotic Arm + Electromagnet
DisposalDirected Atmospheric Reentry

INTELLIGENCE LAYER

AI Camera System

Real-Time Detection

The onboard camera continuously scans the surrounding orbital space, identifying debris objects using trained computer vision models.

⊞

Size Classification

AI algorithms estimate debris dimensions and material composition to decide which capture system to deploy — net or robotic arm.

⊛

Trajectory Prediction

The system calculates debris velocity and trajectory, timing the deployment of the capture system for maximum interception accuracy.

⟳

Autonomous Control

No ground command needed for capture execution. The satellite operates fully autonomously through the detection-to-deorbit pipeline.
