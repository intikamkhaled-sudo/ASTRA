MISSION PHASES

Simulation Breakdown

T-00

Approach & Scan

Satellite maneuvers to intercept zone. AI camera activates and begins orbital debris survey.

T-01

Target Lock

AI identifies and classifies target debris. System selects net or arm based on size analysis.

T-02

Capture

Selected system deploys. Net or electromagnetic arm secures the debris object.

T-03

Deorbit Burn

Onboard thrusters execute deorbit maneuver, reducing orbital velocity to initiate reentry.

T-04

Atmospheric Reentry

Debris enters Earth's atmosphere and disintegrates completely due to aerodynamic heating.
